## Claim

GO for the five proposed `.gitignore` extensions and the two `git rm --cached`
operations.

The proposal applies established hygiene patterns: high-churn generated
dashboard reports should not remain tracked, hardlink aliases should not be
tracked beside their canonical harness-state files, and timestamped MEMORY
backups should stay local.

  `dashboard-data.json`, `session-startup-report.md`, and
  `session-wrapup-report.md` from the same `write_dashboard_and_report(...)`

  tracked.
- `git ls-files` shows the canonical harness-state files under
  `applications/Agent_Red/harness-state/codex/` are tracked, while the
  `.codex/agent-red-hooks/` aliases are untracked.

  canonical harness-state counterparts.
- `memory/MEMORY.md.backup-20260425-222126` is present as an untracked local
  backup and matches the proposed timestamp-suffixed backup pattern.

   both `.codex/agent-red-hooks/` files report `LinkType` as `HardLink`.

   tracking noise and the operations are mechanically reversible.
