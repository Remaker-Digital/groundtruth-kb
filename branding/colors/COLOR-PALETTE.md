## Design Notes

- **Agent Red (`#C41E2A`)** — a classic, mid-deep red that reads as authoritative without feeling alarming. Distinct from common SaaS blues and greens.
- **Charcoal (`#1A1A2E`)** — slight blue-purple undertone that pairs elegantly with red, avoiding the flatness of pure black.
- **Navy (`#1E3A5F`)** — cool secondary accent complementing the warmth of red. Used for links and informational elements where red would be too intense.
- **Crimson (`#D32F2F`)** — deliberately differentiated from brand red to avoid confusion between brand elements and error states.
