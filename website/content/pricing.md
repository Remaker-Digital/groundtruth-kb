## FAQ Section

### Section Headline
**Pricing Questions**

---

#### Q: Is there a free trial?
**A:** Yes! Every plan includes a 14-day free trial with full Professional features. No credit card required to start. At the end of your trial, choose the plan that fits your needs.

---

#### Q: What happens if I exceed my conversation limit?
**A:** We'll notify you at 80% and 90% of your limit. If you exceed your limit, conversations continue at the overage rate for your plan ($0.15, $0.10, or $0.05 per conversation). Enterprise plans have no hard caps.

---

#### Q: Can I change plans anytime?
**A:** Absolutely. Upgrade instantly at any time—we'll prorate the difference. Downgrade at the end of your current billing cycle. No penalties, no hassle.

---

#### Q: Do you offer refunds?
**A:** We offer a full refund within the first 30 days if you're not satisfied. After 30 days, you can cancel anytime but refunds are not available for partial months.

---

#### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and can arrange invoicing for annual Enterprise plans.

---

#### Q: Are there setup fees?
**A:** No setup fees for Starter or Professional plans. Enterprise plans include complimentary custom onboarding. Additional professional services are available if needed.

---

#### Q: Can I get a custom plan?
**A:** Yes. For businesses with unique requirements—very high volume, custom integrations, or specific compliance needs—contact our sales team for a custom quote.

---

#### Q: Do you offer month-to-month billing?
**A:** Yes. All plans are available month-to-month with no long-term commitment required. Annual plans offer significant savings if you're ready to commit.

---

#### Q: What's included in the free trial?
**A:** Full access to Professional plan features for 14 days, including up to 500 conversations, Shopify and Zendesk integrations, and priority support. No credit card required.

---

#### Q: How do I cancel?
**A:** Cancel anytime from your account settings or by contacting support. No penalties, no hassle. Your service continues until the end of your current billing period.
