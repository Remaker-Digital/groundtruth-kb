## How Pricing Works Section

### Section Headline
**Two Simple Components**

### Section Description
Agent Red pricing has two parts. Both are fully transparent.

### Component 1: Platform Fee
Your monthly plan covers the infrastructure, integrations, support, and features that power your AI customer service. This is a fixed monthly cost based on the tier you choose.

### Component 2: AI Conversations
Every plan includes a monthly allowance of AI-resolved conversations. If your customers need more, you pay a simple per-conversation rate — or pre-purchase conversation packs at a discount. You always know the cost before it happens.

### Why This Model
Other platforms hide AI costs inside opaque per-seat fees, charge $0.99+ per resolution, or force you through sales calls for pricing. Agent Red shows you every number. Our AI costs less than $0.01 per conversation to run — and we pass that efficiency to you.

## AI Conversation Pricing Section

### Section Headline
**AI Conversation Costs — Fully Transparent**

### Section Description
Every plan includes a monthly conversation allowance. When you need more, you have two options: pay-as-you-go overage, or pre-purchase conversation packs at a discount. You always know the rate before you use it.

### How We Define a Conversation
An AI conversation is a complete customer interaction that Agent Red resolves without human intervention. This includes intent classification, knowledge retrieval, response generation, safety validation, and escalation checking — the full six-agent pipeline. Conversations that escalate to your human team are **not counted** against your AI allowance.

### Overage Rates (Pay-As-You-Go)

|------|---------------|-------------|---------------------|
| Starter | 1,000 | $0.04/conv | $149 + $40 = $189/mo |
| Professional | 5,000 | $0.025/conv | $399 + $125 = $524/mo |
| Enterprise | 20,000 | $0.015/conv | $999 + $300 = $1,299/mo |

### Conversation Packs (Pre-Purchase & Save)

Pre-purchase conversation blocks at a discount. Packs are valid for 90 days and stack with your included allowance. Available on all tiers.

| Pack | Price | Effective Rate | Best For |
|------|-------|---------------|----------|
| 1,000 conversations | $29 | $0.029/conv | Handling seasonal spikes |
| 5,000 conversations | $99 | $0.020/conv | Steady growth beyond included |
| 20,000 conversations | $249 | $0.012/conv | High-volume operations |

### Usage Alerts
Agent Red notifies you at 50%, 80%, and 95% of your included allowance. No surprise bills — you always have time to purchase a pack or adjust your plan before overage kicks in.

### Why Our AI Costs So Little
Agent Red's six-agent architecture uses purpose-optimized AI models. Lightweight tasks (classification, escalation, analytics) run on efficient models, and only response generation uses a premium model. The result: our AI cost per conversation is under $0.01. We pass that efficiency directly to you.

## FAQ Section

### Section Headline
**Pricing Questions**

---

#### Q: What's included in the free trial?
**A:** Full Professional features for 14 days, including up to 500 AI conversations, Shopify and Zendesk integrations, and priority support. No credit card required. At the end of your trial, choose the plan that fits your needs.

---

#### Q: How do AI conversations work? What counts as one?
**A:** An AI conversation is a complete customer interaction resolved by Agent Red without human intervention. It includes all six agents working together — intent classification, knowledge retrieval, response generation, safety validation, and escalation checking. If a conversation escalates to your human team, it is **not** counted against your AI conversation allowance.

---

#### Q: What happens when I exceed my included conversations?
**A:** Agent Red notifies you at 50%, 80%, and 95% of your monthly allowance — no surprise bills. Beyond your included conversations, you pay the overage rate for your tier ($0.04, $0.025, or $0.015 per conversation). You can also pre-purchase conversation packs at a discount to cover expected spikes.

---

#### Q: Why is Agent Red so much cheaper than competitors?
**A:** Three reasons. First, our six-agent architecture uses purpose-optimized AI models — lightweight models for classification and routing, premium models only for response generation. Second, our infrastructure runs on Azure Container Apps with auto-scaling, so we don't pay for idle capacity. Third, we pass our efficiency to you instead of charging $0.99+ per resolution like some competitors.

---

#### Q: Can I change plans anytime?
**A:** Yes. Upgrade instantly at any time — we prorate the difference. Downgrade at the end of your current billing cycle. Add or remove add-ons anytime. No penalties, no lock-in.

---

#### Q: Do escalated conversations count against my allowance?
**A:** No. Only conversations fully resolved by AI count. If a customer is escalated to your human team via Zendesk (or email), that conversation does not count against your included allowance and is not billed as overage.

---

#### Q: Are add-ons required?
**A:** No. Every base plan is complete and fully functional on its own. Add-ons are optional enhancements — you can add them when your business needs them and remove them when it doesn't.

---

#### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover). Annual plans on Enterprise tier are available via invoice and ACH/wire transfer.

---

#### Q: Is there a setup fee?
**A:** No setup fees on any plan. Enterprise includes a complimentary custom onboarding program. All other plans include self-service onboarding with documentation and guides.

---

#### Q: Do you offer refunds?
**A:** Yes. 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If Agent Red isn't right for your business within the first 30 days, you get a full refund — no questions asked.

---

#### Q: What personalization is included in my plan?
**A:** Every plan includes Customer Context (structured profile injection) and Conversation Memory (semantic search across interaction history) at no additional cost. Professional and Enterprise plans add Cross-Session Learning, which extracts and persists customer preferences and patterns over time. Enterprise customers can optionally add Dedicated Model Training ($299/month) for per-customer AI fine-tuning on 1,000+ interactions.

---

#### Q: How does the annual discount work?
**A:** Annual billing saves 17% (equivalent to 2 months free). You pay once upfront for 12 months at the discounted rate. The 30-day money-back guarantee still applies. Add-ons purchased during an annual plan are billed monthly.
