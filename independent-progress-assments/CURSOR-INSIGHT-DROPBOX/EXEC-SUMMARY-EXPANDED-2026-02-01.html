1. Who Is the Customer?

Primary Customer Segment

E-commerce merchants, with emphasis on Shopify stores, who need AI-powered customer support to scale beyond manual ticket handling.

Ideal Customer Profile

  1. Size: SMB to mid-market — from solo operators (1–5 person teams) to businesses handling 1,000–20,000+ support conversations per month
  2. Platform: Primarily Shopify; secondary: BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Magento, custom platforms
  3. Pain points: High volume, repetitive inquiries, unpredictable AI costs from incumbents, desire for faster response times
  4. Budget: $149–$999/month with predictable per-conversation overage ($0.04–$0.015 by tier)
  5. Evaluation: 14-day trials; compare against Tidio, Gorgias, Zendesk, Intercom, Re:amaze

Secondary Segments

  • Non-Shopify e-commerce: Reached via direct Stripe billing
  • Agencies and resellers: Future white-label capability
  • Enterprise: Dedicated model fine-tuning ($299/mo add-on), 99.95% SLA, unlimited history depth

2. What Customer Problems Does It Solve?

2.1 Cost Predictability and Alignment

Problem: Incumbent tools charge per-seat and per-AI-resolution; bills rise with AI success.

Solution: Flat platform fee + transparent per-conversation overage. AI included in all tiers. Allowances 1K/5K/20K; overage $0.04–$0.015. Pre-purchase packs (1K/$29, 5K/$99, 20K/$249).

2.2 Personalization Without Custom Engineering

Problem: Generic AI feels impersonal; per-customer context usually requires custom engineering.

Solution: Four-layer Persistent Customer Memory:

  1. Layer 1 (All tiers): Structured profile from 6 data sources (~250 tokens)
  2. Layer 2 (All tiers): Vector RAG over conversation history (text-embedding-3-large, Cosmos DB DiskANN); tier depth 90d/365d/unlimited
  3. Layer 3 (Professional+): Cross-session pattern extraction with confidence and decay
  4. Layer 4 (Enterprise add-on): Per-merchant fine-tuning on 1,000+ conversations ($299/mo)

No competitor has confirmed per-customer vector RAG over historical transcripts.

2.3 Safety and Compliance

Problem: AI can generate inappropriate or non-compliant content.

Solution: Fail-closed Critic/Supervisor validates every response. Safe fallback on reject/timeout/error. Circuit breakers; per-response explainability traces.

2.4 Multi-Channel Distribution

Solution: Dual-channel — Shopify App Store (primary) plus Stripe direct. Unified tenant provisioning; channel-specific billing.
