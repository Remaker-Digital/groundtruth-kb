## Introducing Persistent Customer Memory

Persistent Customer Memory (PCM) is the breakthrough at the heart of Agent Red. It's a four-layer personalization architecture that gives your AI support agent something no competitor offers: the ability to build genuine, lasting relationships with your customers.

**Layer 1: Customer Context**
From the very first interaction, Agent Red builds a structured profile for each customer — their preferences, account status, purchase history, and interaction patterns. This context is injected into every conversation, so the AI always knows who it's talking to. Cost: essentially zero.

**Layer 2: Conversation Memory**
Every conversation is vectorized and stored using semantic embeddings. When a customer returns, Agent Red performs a hybrid search across their entire conversation history — combining keyword matching with meaning-based retrieval. The result? The AI recalls not just what was said, but the context and sentiment behind it. Retrieval happens in under 50 milliseconds. Cost: roughly one cent per customer per month.

**Layer 3: Cross-Session Learning**
For Professional and Enterprise customers, Agent Red goes further. It extracts behavioral patterns, communication preferences, and recurring needs from the full conversation history. The AI learns that a particular customer prefers concise answers, or that another always asks about gift wrapping options. These patterns update automatically with every new interaction.

**Layer 4: Dedicated Model Training**
For Enterprise clients, Agent Red offers per-customer AI fine-tuning. After 1,000+ interactions, we train a dedicated model that captures the nuances of your highest-value customer relationships — their vocabulary, their expectations, their communication style. This is personalization that no competitor can match, because no competitor has the architecture to deliver it.

## Not One AI. Six.

Most chatbot platforms run a single language model and hope for the best. Agent Red uses six purpose-built AI agents that work together in a coordinated pipeline — each one researched, designed, tested, and fine-tuned for a specific job:

**1. Intent Classification Agent**
Before generating a single word of response, Agent Red identifies exactly what your customer needs. Our classifier recognizes 17+ intent categories — from order status and returns to product questions and complaints — with 98%+ accuracy in under 200 milliseconds.

**2. Knowledge Retrieval Agent**
Once intent is established, this agent searches your product catalog, knowledge base, policy documents, and conversation history using hybrid semantic search. It doesn't just find keyword matches — it understands meaning, so "I want to send this back" retrieves your return policy even if the word "return" never appears.

**3. Response Generation Agent**
With intent classified and knowledge retrieved, this agent crafts a response in your brand's exact voice. Choose from Professional, Friendly, Casual, or Formal tone profiles, or define your own. Every response reflects your brand personality — not generic chatbot language.

**4. Critic and Supervisor Agent**
Before any response reaches your customer, it passes through our safety layer. The Critic scans for prompt injection attacks, PII leakage, hallucinated information, competitor mentions, and compliance violations. It catches problems that would damage your brand, with a 100% block rate on malicious inputs.

**5. Escalation Agent**
Some conversations need a human touch. The Escalation Agent evaluates confidence scores, customer sentiment, and issue complexity to make intelligent routing decisions. When it escalates, it routes to the right team with full context — so your human agents never start from scratch. Escalation precision: 100%.

**6. Analytics Agent**
Every conversation feeds real-time analytics: volume, intent distribution, resolution rates, response times, satisfaction scores, and cost per conversation. You see exactly how your AI is performing and where to improve.

**Why does this matter?** Because a single monolithic model is a single point of failure. Our six-agent architecture means each component can be independently tested, tuned, and upgraded without affecting the others. When we improve our intent classifier, your response quality doesn't change. When we upgrade the response generator, your safety filters stay rock-solid.

We don't just deploy AI and walk away. Each agent is continuously evaluated against rigorous quality benchmarks — 4,522 unit tests, 917 UI tests, 25 conversation quality scenarios, and 21 critical-path integration tests, all passing with zero failures. This is the engineering discipline that separates a production platform from a demo.
