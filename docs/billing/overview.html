Billing & Usage

Agent Red uses a transparent pricing model combining a fixed platform fee with metered AI usage. This page explains how billing works.

Your Subscription Plan​

Your plan determines your monthly platform fee, included conversation allowance, overage rate, and available features.

TierMonthlyIncluded ConversationsOverage Rate
Starter$149/mo1,000/mo$0.04/conv
Professional$399/mo5,000/mo$0.025/conv
Enterprise$999/mo20,000/mo$0.015/conv

Annual billing saves 17% on platform fees.

How Conversations Are Billed​

Billing follows a three-tier priority:

  1. Included allowance — Conversations are first consumed from your plan's monthly included allowance
  2. Conversation packs — After included allowance is exhausted, pre-purchased pack credits are consumed (FIFO order)
  3. Overage — Conversations beyond both included and pack balance are billed at your tier's overage rate

See Billable Conversation Spec for the exact definition of what counts as a billable conversation.

Conversation Packs​

Pre-purchase conversation credits at discounted rates. Packs are consumed after your included allowance and before overage billing.

PackPriceEffective RateValidity
1,000 conversations$29$0.029/conv90 days
5,000 conversations$99$0.020/conv90 days
20,000 conversations$249$0.012/conv90 days

Packs are consumed in FIFO (first-in, first-out) order. Unused credits expire after 90 days.

Usage Dashboard​

The Billing page shows real-time usage for your current billing period:

  • Conversations used — Total billable conversations with a usage ring showing percentage of included allowance consumed
  • Pack balance — Remaining pre-purchased conversation credits
  • Current overage — Dollar amount of overage charges accrued this period
  • Estimated overage cost — Projected total overage cost based on current usage trends
  • Daily usage chart — 30-day view of total and billable conversations per day

Managing Your Subscription​

Subscription changes (upgrades, downgrades, cancellation) are managed through the billing portal. Contact support for custom Enterprise pricing or volume discounts.
